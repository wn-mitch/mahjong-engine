# American NMJL 2026 — Strategy Guide

Verified card transcription and flexibility-first strategy framework. Tuned for **unscored play** (pure win/lose per round).

For universal mahjong concepts (joker rules, concealed hands, Charleston mechanics), see [concepts.md](concepts.md).

## Strategic Stance for Unscored Play

Two shifts from default scored strategy:

- **Skip all concealed (C-marked) hands.** No scoring means no concealed premium — concealed is pure handicap. This eliminates 9 hands from consideration.
- **Call exposures aggressively.** Concealment penalty doesn't exist; speed dominates.

## Universal Tile Hierarchy

Rank-order of which individual tiles are most valuable to hold during the Charleston:

1. **Jokers** — always #1
2. **Flowers (FF/FFF/FFFF/FFFFFF)** — appear in 27 of the 34 playable hands
3. **White dragons** — appear in 2026 hands AND wild-zero AND in any dragon group
4. **6s** — appear in 2026, 2468, 369, and Consec Runs covering 6 (highest non-honor cross-section)
5. **2s** — 2026, 2468, Winds-Dragons even hand, Consec Runs containing 2
6. **Red/Green dragons** — usable in any DDD/DDDD group across many hands
7. **NEWS (any wind)** — Winds-Dragons section, 22 00 222 666 NEWS, 33 66 666 999 NEWS

**Rule**: never pass jokers, flowers, or white dragons during Charleston. Pass last: 6s, 2s, colored dragons. Pass first: isolated odd numbers if you're playing the evens family.

## Pre-Charleston Triage

1. **Sort tiles by family.** Count evens, odds, honors, jokers, flowers, dragons.
2. **Identify your dominant family.** Pick whichever has the most tiles.
3. **Pick 3 candidate hands within that family.** Don't pick one — keep three live.
4. **Default candidate sets:**
   - **Evens dominant** → `222 444 6666 8888` (2468), `222 000 2222 6666` (2026), `333 666 6666 9999` (369). All three share 6s.
   - **Odds dominant** → `111 333 3333 5555` (13579), `1111 FFFFFF 1111` (Like Numbers), `NNN 1111 1111 SSS` (Winds-Dragons). All three share odd-number kongs.
   - **Honors dominant** → `NNNN EEE WWW SSSS`, `FFF NNNN FFF DDDD`, `EEE 2222 2222 WWW` (Winds-Dragons trio).
   - **Consecutive cluster** (3-4 adjacent numbers) → `111 222 3333 4444`, `1111 FFFFFF 2222`, `FF 1111 2222 3333`.
5. **Track jokers separately** — 2+ jokers in starting hand opens the Quints section as a serious target.

## Charleston Decisions

### Always pass
- Singletons in suits/families you've abandoned
- Isolated numbers outside your family
- Tiles useful for only one candidate hand if you have 3 of that tile already

### Never pass
- Jokers
- Flowers
- White dragons (if playing evens family — they're 0-wild)
- Any pair
- The 6 most-needed tiles for your top candidate

### Pass timing
- **First pass (right)**: dump clearest non-fits
- **Second pass (across)**: continue trimming
- **Third pass (left)**: commit to family; pass anything outside it
- **Courtesy (across)**: only pass if you have 2-3 obvious dead tiles

## In-Round Play

1. Call any discard that advances your hand to a fully joker-friendly target.
2. Spend jokers immediately when they fit a committed group. Don't hoard.
3. Watch exposures — if opponents are also working evens-family hands, the 6s will dry up. Pivot to 369 or Winds-Dragons even hand.
4. By turn 8-10, you should have 2 candidate hands narrowed to 1 primary + 1 backup. Both should share at least 8 tiles.
5. When dead, defensive play: discard tiles already in the discard pool or absent from all exposures.

## Cheat Sheet

**Always hold**: jokers, flowers, white dragons, your dominant family's tiles, any pair.

**Top 5 hands to aim for (most joker-friendly + most overlap):**
1. `222 444 6666 8888` (2468, 1-2 Suits) — evens family anchor
2. `333 666 6666 9999` (369, 2-3 Suits) — evens family pivot via 6s
3. `222 000 2222 6666` (2026, 2 Suits) — evens + white dragons
4. `NNNN EEE WWW SSSS` (Winds-Dragons) — honors family anchor
5. `111 222 3333 4444` (Consec Run, 1-2 Suits) — consecutive family anchor

**Skip entirely**: all C-marked hands (Singles & Pairs section, plus 8 individual hands in other sections).

**Pivot rules**:
- 2026 → 2468 (share 2s, 6s)
- 2468 → 369 (share 6s)
- Consec Run line 4 (`111 222 3333 4444`) → can sub any 4 adjacent numbers; very flexible internally
- Winds-Dragons → most hands shareable through honors
